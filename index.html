ABOUT

Hi! My name is Karo. I'm a 17-year-old software engineer with 6 years of hobbyist experience.

I'm a passionate software engineer with a strong belief in the value of continuously learning new things, especially in secondary or unrelated fields like physics, artistry and journalism.

I'm also a privacy nerd, who believes that everybody should have the right to their own data, and that all core technologies should be open-source.

Currently, I'm fascinated by artificial intelligence and its potential impact on society, which is why I enjoy dedicating time towards building or maintaining open-source AI tools.
