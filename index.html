ABOUT

Hi! I'm Karo. I'm a 17-year-old who's been learning to code, draw, and write for the past 6 years.

I'm a passionate towards software engineering with a strong belief in the value of continuously learning new things, especially in secondary or unrelated fields like physics, artistry and journalism.

Being a privacy nerd, I think that everybody should have the right to their own data & life and that all core technologies should be open-source.

I'm a big machine learning guy. I especially enjoy learning about transformer-based architectures or language modeling AI. I continuously work on new ML projects, whether they're chess bots or tiny language models with custom architectures.

PROJECTS

All of my (open-source) projects are available on my GitHub.
